The Challenge: Complexity in Distributed Systems

Modern systems often suffer from fragmentation, inconsistency, ambiguity, and inefficient GitOps practices. ONDEMANDENV addresses these head-on.

Fragmentation & Monolithic Environments

Services might be small, but they often share rigid dev/qa/prod environments, hindering independent deployment and creating bottlenecks. Infrastructure and application configurations become scattered and disconnected.

Inconsistency

Configuration drift between environments and services leads to unreliable deployments and "works on my machine" issues.

Ambiguity

Implicit dependencies and unclear ownership between services and infrastructure cause confusion and slow down development.

YAML/GitOps Trap

Over-reliance on static YAML and container-centric tools often fails to manage the complete application lifecycle and its dependencies effectively.

How ONDEMANDENV Helps

ONDEMANDENV provides a unified platform based on codified contracts (`contractsLib`), versioned environments (`Envers`), and on-demand cloning.

Define contracts, and the platform orchestrates infrastructure, dependencies, and deployments across your AWS accounts.
